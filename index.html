Viewer for the SNCF permanent railway signalling open data, with OSM integration via JOSM or clipboard copy.

How to use

Zoom in to load signals. At low zoom only major signal types are shown; zoom ≥ 10 displays all types in the viewport. Click any marker to view its properties and export OSM tags to JOSM or the clipboard.
